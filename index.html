📐 How Combined Ratings Work
The VA uses the Whole Person Method. Each rating applies to the remaining "able" percentage.

Example: 50% + 30% → 30% of remaining 50% = 15%. Combined = 65%, rounds to 70%.
🏁 Rating Milestones
10%Compensation begins
30%Dependent allowances
50%CHAMPVA eligibility
70%TDIU threshold
100%Full + P&T 🎯
