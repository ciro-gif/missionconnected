Privacy Policy

Mission: Connected is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What We Collect

We collect only what you provide: name, email, phone, state, birth month/year, and your VA screener answers. No sensitive financial data is ever collected.

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to generate and save your personalized VA disability roadmap. It is never sold, rented, shared, or used for advertising — ever.

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest via Supabase infrastructure. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

Your Rights

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with the VA, insurance companies, attorneys, advertisers, or any other third party. Period.

Building Your Roadmap
Analyzing your service history and symptoms…
⚖️
Service Connection — The 3 Elements
Every VA disability claim requires three things: (1) a current diagnosis, (2) an in-service event or exposure, and (3) a medical nexus linking the two. Miss any one and VA can deny. Your roadmap will show you exactly what each condition needs.
🔗
Secondary Service Connection
Once you win a primary claim, new conditions caused or worsened by that rated condition can be filed as secondary. For example: sleep apnea secondary to asthma, or hypertension secondary to PTSD. Burden of proof is lower — the anchor condition does the legal work.
⚡
PACT Act (2022)
The PACT Act expanded VA benefits for veterans exposed to burn pits, Agent Orange, radiation, and other toxic hazards. For covered conditions, VA presumes service connection — meaning you don't need to prove causation. VA must schedule a C&P exam at no cost to you.
🔬
TERA — Toxic Exposure Risk Activity
TERA is a VA program recognizing toxic training exposures — like chemical agents used in CBRN training. It's not the same as PACT Act presumptives. Under TERA, VA has a duty to consider your documented exposure when evaluating your claim. A specialist nexus letter strengthens TERA-based claims significantly.
📋
Intent to File — Protect Your Date
Filing an Intent to File (ITF) costs nothing and locks your effective date — the date VA starts calculating back pay. Do it immediately, even before you have all your evidence. You have 12 months to submit your full claim. Every day you wait could mean lost retroactive pay.
🩺
The C&P Exam — Your Most Important Day
The Compensation & Pension exam is where VA examiners evaluate your symptoms. Describe your worst days — not your average days. The rating schedule is based on severity. If you minimize your symptoms that day, your rating reflects it permanently. You're not exaggerating — you're being accurate.
